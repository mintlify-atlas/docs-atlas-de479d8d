## Key Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Service Ticket Tracking" icon="clipboard-list">
    Track repairs from intake to delivery with customizable status workflows, automatic folio generation, and customer notifications. Generate PDF service tickets and QR codes for easy status lookup.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Multi-Payment POS System" icon="credit-card">
    Accept cash, card, and transfer payments with real-time stock updates. Support for mobile barcode scanning, price comparison with online marketplaces, and automatic invoice generation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customer Loyalty Program" icon="gift">
    Reward repeat customers with points on every purchase. Track client history, manage contact information, and view complete service and purchase records.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cash Register Operations" icon="money-bill">
    Manage daily cash flow with opening balances, expense tracking, and end-of-day reports. Generate detailed PDF summaries for accounting and audit purposes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
